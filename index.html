Privacy Policy for Finger Abacus

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Finger Abacus — a fun and educational app designed to help children and learners improve their mental math and abacus skills. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Finger Abacus does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. We do not track your activity, device information, location, or any personally identifiable details.

3. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be child-friendly and suitable for users under 13 years of age. We strictly follow COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) guidelines and do not collect or process any data from children or any users.

4. In-App Purchases

Finger Abacus may offer optional premium features through Google Play In-App Purchases. All payment transactions are securely processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment-related information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play Services solely to support in-app purchases and app functionality. No personal data is shared with us or any other third party.

6. Security

Although our app does not collect personal data, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure environment for all users. All communications with Google Play Services are handled through secure and encrypted connections provided by Google.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
